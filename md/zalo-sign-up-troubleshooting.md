Many people report that they have issues signing up for Zalo. It will usually request that you call the hotline or that you ask a Vietnamese friend to help you. Here are some things that you can try:

1. Try signing up with a SIM from a different phone provider instead.
2. Try an online service that lets you receive SMS online. This may or may not work for Zalo (haven't tried it personally). There are many such sites like smspva.
3. Join a local expat group on FB like <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/expatshcmc" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Expats in Ho Chi Minh City"</a> and see if anyone is willing to help you.
